Evrisko Systems Product Overview


 Evrisko Systems has formed a marketing and sales agreement with Veris Industries.  Evrisko WiFi products are now exculsively available from Veris.  Please see Veris' web site: www.veris.com and click on wireless products for ordering information.

Evrisko Systems wireless building control system has the following advantages over other systems:
  • Low cost: The wireless system can be placed throughout the building without running any expensive wires.  System utilizes the existing WiFi network infrastructure already found in most company buildings, schools, hospitals, and homes.  Wi-Fi equipment (routers, access points, antennas, etc) is available from many suppliers and at reasonable costs.  There is no need to add extra equipment to support the Evrisko Systems wireless building control system.
  • Adaptable: Sensors and controls can be easily moved when the building or organizational structure changes.  New sensors are easily added and existing sensors quickly reprogrammed to adapt to the changes.  Wi-Fi networks are highly scalable; many sensors can be added without any impact on current uses.
  • Plug and Play: Sensors are easy to configure and automatically connect the existing network.  Homeowners should be able to self install.
  • Ultra Low power: Using new Wi-Fi technology that allows very low power communications, the Evrisko Systems sensors and controls will last up to 5 years on a pair of AA batteries (actual battery life will depend on the sensor and sampling and communications rate).
  • Open standards: The system uses non-proprietary open Internet standards.
  • Secure: Standard Wi-Fi encryption is supported.
