Evrisko Systems Engineering Services

Wireless Projects

Evrisko Systems specializes in the design of low-power WiFi devices.  These devices use custom hardware and firmware design with a commercial off-the-shelf radio module.  End-user tools have been developed for device configuration and monitoring.

While the company’s main focus is with WiFi radios, our engineers have experience with other radio technologies, including ZigBee, Bluetooth, proprietary 900MHz and 2.4GHz radio modules.

Hardware Design

Evrisko Systems engineers have experience in analog and digital design, logic design and synthesis, switching power supply and sensor circuits, PC Board (PCB) design and layout, RF and antenna design.

Evrisko Systems has developed microcontroller and microprocessor designs interfacing with external devices through I2C, SPI, Serial, and other interfaces.  We have experience powering these devices with batteries and a variety of external power sources.

PC Board (PCB) design and layout is done with a variety of tools including OrCad, KiCAD, and EAGLE.  Evrisko Systems works with a local contract manufacturer for the production of final products while many prototype boards are assembled in-house.

Software / Firmware Development

Evrisko Systems engineers have experience developing software on a wide variety of platforms and in many programming languages.  We have experience with microprocessors, real-time programming, and graphical user interfaces.

Evrisko Systems has developed complete custom firmware for our WiFi products and software tools used to support device configuration and monitoring.  Our software is designed to maintain firmware and tool compatibility across all products.  Software languages include Java, C, C++, .Net, Python, Perl.

Database Development

Evrisko Systems uses SQL databases for product configuration and monitoring software.  We have expertise in complex database development.

Niagara Certified Developer     

Evrisko Systems has received certification from Tridium for Niagara AX driver and software development.  We have developed a custom Tridium JACE driver for our WiFi products.

Testing and Validation

Evrisko Systems has developed fully automated functional testing procedures for our products with a custom test station and test fixtures.

Validation testing includes power supply, accelerated battery life, and range testing.  Products are designed to meet emissions (FCC), safety (UL), and environmental (ROHS) guidelines.  Evrisko Systems has experience with testing labs to obtain the necessary certifications.

Product / Project Management

Evrisko Systems has successfully delivered several products to market.  We have experience using project management software and techniques.  By creating design specifications, we ensure that the end-users needs are met without compromise.  We also have the ability to develop hardware and corresponding software at the same time.
