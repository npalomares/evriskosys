JACE Driver Software

All Evrisko / Veris sensors can communicate with a Niagara Ax device using a specially developed driver which is free and easy to install.  Sensors can then communicate with all other devices in your building automation system.

Setting up the devices is easy with the included ACT Software.  Just enter the network information, plug in the USB cable and your device is ready to be deployed.
