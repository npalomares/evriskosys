Network Wireless Gateway
The Aerospond NWGWA2 Network Wireless Gateway is designed to receive wireless (WiFi) signals from the Veris Aerospond wirless sensors (the HWXW series humidity senosrs and the TWXWXX temperature sensor).  These sensors monitor the interior temperature and humidity condiations and convert the data into a wireless signal.  The NWGWA2 receives the signal and convers it to a 4-20 mA, 0-10 VDC, or 0-5 VDC output.  The Network Wireless Gateway also can use the digital ouputs to alarm for a low battery condition on these sensors.

The Network Wireless Gateway has 8 analog outputs (4-20 mA or 0-5 VDC or 0-10 VDC selectable) and 8 digital solid state contact outputs.

Demand Response Relay - Coming 1Q2014
The Demand Response Relay will turn on/off 120V-240V devices.  Designed to allow building owners to respond to peak energy periods by turning high energy devices off by sending a wireless WiFi signal.

The Demand Response Relay will be controlled by a Niagara AX JACE.
